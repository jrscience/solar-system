KUIPER BELT

The Kuiper Belt is a large belt of asteroids found in the outer reaches of the Solar System, past the orbit of Neptune.


WHAT IS THE KUIPER BELT?

  • Pronounced "k-y-per", this is another belt of asteroids found in the Solar System. It is similar to the Asteroid Belt between Mars and Jupiter, but much larger and more massive, and lying past the orbit of Neptune, in the outer Solar System.
  • In addition, while asteroids in the Asteroid Belt tend to be metallic or rocky, most Kuiper belt objects are made up of what scientists call "ices". This can be water ice, but also includes compounds such as methane and ammonia. These compounds are volatile (they evaporate easily.)
